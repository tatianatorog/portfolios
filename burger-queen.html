Burger Queen

Burger Queen is a Progressive Web App (PWA) designed for internal use by the staff of a small burger restaurant. It is a web-based application that can be installed on mobile devices and accessed offline. The interface is simple and easy to use, it allows users to take orders, and send them to the kitchen. This helps to improve efficiency and accuracy in the ordering process, and it also frees up staff to focus on other tasks.

  • Increased efficiency
  • Improved accuracy
  • Increased customer satisfaction
